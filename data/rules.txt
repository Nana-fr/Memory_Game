<h3>Rules</h3><br>
<h6>You have to find each complementary images to form a pair. When all the pairs have been found you win.</h6>
<p class="text-decoration-underline">3 levels of difficulty:</p>
<ul>
<li>Easy: You can make as many moves as you want.</li>
<li>Medium: You have to find all the pairs in 12 moves max.</li>
<li>Difficult: You have to find all the pairs in 9 moves max.</li>
</ul>
<p class="text-decoration-underline">There are also 3 themes available:</p>
<ul>
<li>Chinese: Find the pinyin corresponding to the character</li>
<li>Japonese: Find the kunyomi pronunciation of the kanji</li>
<li>Colors: Find the color corresponding to the text</li>
</ul>
